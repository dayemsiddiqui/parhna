What is parhna

The purpose of parhna is to make education easy, free and accessible for everyone around the world. Its like the Holy Grail of open learning because it allows everyone and anyone to be either a teacher or a student, all for free. We provide a stable and robust platform to everybody around the world to teach, learn and grow.

How does it work

Its very simple to get started will parhna. Simply sign up for free and start learning or teaching. We provide a platform with features like online collaborative whiteboards, quiz creation and testing system, peer evaluation, collaborative presentation and slides, classroom creation and lots of other features.

Our goal is to enable everyone across the world to learn, share and teach. We want to revolutionize the future of education by shifting the experience from a typical classroom environment to an online platform.
